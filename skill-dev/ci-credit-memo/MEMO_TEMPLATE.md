## Notes on Using This Template:

3. **Add sections as needed** for unique situations

6. **Use tables for financial data** - easier to read than paragraphs
7. **Be concise but complete** - credit committees appreciate brevity with substance
8. **Focus on decision-making** - what does the reader need to know to approve/decline?
9. **Balance positives and negatives** - objective analysis builds credibility
10. **Make the recommendation clear** - no ambiguity on whether to approve
